Project

Heat transfer analysis through phase change materials(PCM)

The influence of mass flow rate of heat transfer fluid on parrafin wax and parrafin wax with mesh is examined for both melting and solidification process.
After introduction of aluminium mesh it has been observed that PCM has taken 160min of time to melt, before introducing mesh it required 255 minutes
of time there by 37.5% of time has been saved and this shows increase in latent heat storage capacity.


Experimental Setup comprises of a Blower attached with Heater, which forces hot air to pass to the test section via orifice.
Orifice is incorporated so as to quantify the flow rate of the air. Ends of Orifice are connected to U-Tube Manometer for recording the difference in air head.
A flow regulating valve is placed in between Heater and Orifice for regulating the flow of air.
